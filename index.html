About

The secret to doing anything is believing that you can do it. Anything that you believe you can do strong enough, you can do. Anything. As long as you believe. I guess I'm a little weird. I like to talk to trees and animals. That's okay though; I have more fun than most people. We're trying to teach you a technique here and how to use it. No pressure. Just relax and watch it happen. Paint anything you want on the canvas. Create your own world. Maybe there's a happy little Evergreen that lives here.

Let all these things just sort of happen.

You don't want to kill all your dark areas they are very important. Go out on a limb - that's where the fruit is.

  • You can create anything that makes you happy.
  • We'll put all the little clouds in and let them dance around and have fun.
  • We must be quiet, soft and gentle.
  • Use your imagination, let it go.
  • Do almighty paintings with us!
